Polygon Full Stack
One Integration, Complete Coverage

When you use Polygon for everything, you get a single vendor relationship that covers the full US crypto stack — from user onboarding through transaction settlement. No need to coordinate between separate KYC, payment, and custody providers. One API, one contract, one integration.

How It Works: Full Stack Flow
A new user initiates a transaction in your app. Polygon runs non-documentary KYC screening in the background. The user pays via Apple Pay, Google Pay, debit card, ACH, or physical cash — all processed natively by Polygon. Polygon converts the settled USD to the target asset and delivers it to a Polygon-provisioned custodial wallet. Your platform receives a webhook confirmation.

Every step — KYC, payment authorization, conversion, and custody — is handled by Polygon under a single API integration. Polygon also provides a chargeback guarantee on all card onramp transactions and access to 50,000+ cash and ATM locations nationwide.
  • 🔗
    Single Vendor, Zero Coordination
    No need to manage separate relationships with a KYC provider, payment processor, and custody platform. Polygon is the one partner for everything.
  • 🛡️
    Chargeback Guarantee Included
    Polygon absorbs chargeback liability on Apple Pay, Google Pay, and debit card onramp — a significant risk removed from your payment stack.
  • 💵
    Physical Cash Built In
    50,000+ retail and ATM locations for cash-preferred and unbanked users — a channel that modular-only integrations can't easily replicate.
Bring Your Own Providers
Plug Polygon Into Your Existing Stack

If you already have a KYC vendor, payment processor, or wallet infrastructure, you don't need to replace them. Polygon's API is modular — use Polygon for conversion and licensing, and keep your existing providers for everything else. Pass verified KYC data via API at no fee, route settled funds from your processor to Polygon for conversion, and have converted assets delivered to your own wallets or directly to a user's external wallet.

How It Works: Modular Flow
A user completes KYC through your existing provider. You pass the verified data to Polygon via API — no additional screening fee. The user pays through your payment processor, and you route the settled USD to Polygon. Polygon converts the funds to the target asset and delivers to your wallet infrastructure or the user's external wallet. Your platform receives a webhook confirmation.

Polygon handles conversion and regulatory licensing (37 MTLs, 48 states) — your partner doesn't need to apply for or maintain US crypto licenses regardless of which modules they use.
  • 🧩
    Keep What's Already Working
    No need to rip out existing vendors. If your KYC, payments, or wallets are already in place, keep them and add Polygon for what's missing.
  • 📤
    Flexible Wallet Delivery
    Deliver converted assets to your own wallet infrastructure or directly to a user's existing external wallet — same API either way.
  • 🏛️
    Licensing Always Included
    Regardless of which modules you use, Polygon's 37 MTLs and 48-state coverage mean you don't need your own US crypto licenses.
